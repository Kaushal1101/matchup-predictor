Fields
A) Batsman
1. Career Average
2. Career Strike rate
3. Handedness (R/L)
4. Match score
5. Match strike rate
6. Match balls faced
7. Partnership score difference (batsman score - non-striker score) [NOT IN MVP]
8. Partnership strike rate difference (batsman SR - non-striker SR) [NOT IN MVP]
9. Career Average vs Country [NOT IN MVP]
10. Career Strike Rate vs Country [NOT IN MVP]

B) Bowler
1. Career Bowling Average
2. Career Bowling Strike Rate 
4. Handedness
5. Bowling style
6. Match Wickets 
7. Match Runs Conceded
8. Match Overs Bowled
9. Career Bowling Average vs Country [NOT IN MVP]
10. Career Bowling Strike Rate vs Country [NOT IN MVP]

C) Match
1. isChase
2. Target (if chasing) [NOT IN MVP]
3. First innings runs scored (if chasing) [NOT IN MVP]
4. Runs remaining (if chasing)
5. Required run rate (if chasing)
6. Balls Bowled (can divide by 120)
7. Runs scored
8. Run rate
9. Wickets fallen
10. Venue (Ground) [NOT IN MVP]
11. Venue (Country)
12. isBattingHome (if batting side is playing at home country) [NOT IN MVP]
13. isBowlingHome (same as isBattingHome but for bowling) [NOT IN MVP]
14. isNeutral (if neither team home game) [NOT IN MVP]
15. Year
